How to Go Contactless

California is making it easier for public transit providers to accept contactless credit/debit/prepaid cards or mobile wallets on a smart device through Master Service Agreements (MSAs) established by the California Department of General Services (DGS). Transit prividers can take advantage of the MSAs to purchase the key hardware and software needed to accept contactless payment.

Reach out to us for support at any time as your agency explore these resources.
